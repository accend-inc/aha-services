            body="<p>AT2320 supports several depreciation methods for Classic Asset processing, Straight Line (SL) being the most commonly used. The SL method distributes depreciation evenly throughout the life of the asset. However, when subsequent cost basis additions are made (after the initial addition / purchase), BOLO essentially extends the useful life of the asset because the new addition uses the same / original useful life but starting from a later date. We need a new depreciation method that works like SL depreciation (distributing the new additions evenly) but keeps the useful life \"fixed\" rather than extending it. This approach is commonly called \"SL over Remaining Useful Life.\"</p>"
